Image Restoration Evaluation

In this study, we are evaluating different AI methods for restoring degraded images. Here's what you need to know:

  • The reference image is the person's original high-quality image.
  • We have a low-quality version of this image that has been restored using nine different AI methods.
  • Your task is to evaluate how well each method restored the image.

What to Look For:

Please rank the images based on these three important factors:

  1. Similarity to Original: How closely the restored image matches the reference image
  2. Identity Preservation: How well the person's facial features and identity are maintained
  3. Overall Quality: The general quality and natural appearance of the restored image

How to Rank:

  1. Click on the image that you think is the best restoration first (this will be ranked as 1).
  2. Continue clicking on images in order of quality, with the least successful restoration being ranked as 9.
  3. If you want to change your ranking, simply click on an image again to deselect it.

Comparing Images:

  • Use the slider below each image to compare it with the reference image.
  • Slide left to see the reference image, slide right to see the restored image.
  • This can help you make more accurate comparisons of the restoration quality.

Note: Submission is only possible after all 20 pages have been completed.

Thank you for your participation!
